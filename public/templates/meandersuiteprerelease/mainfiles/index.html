About Meander Suite

Meander Suite is a complex and revolutionary research application developed by Nielsen Innovations that specialises in childrens behaviours and specifically how weather effects these behaviours. This app takes a researched and tested response to help teachers be more informed about the behaviours of their students. The revolutionary new CWIS Model provides all of the information teachers require to Predict, Prepare, and Prevent negative behaviour incidents. Meaning less time spent managing bad behaviors, and more time spent learning.

What is the CWIS Index?

The Climatic Weather Impact Score (CWIS) Index quantifies the influence of weather on children’s behavior. By analyzing key atmospheric variables, the CWIS Index produces a severity score from 0–100, giving educators, caregivers, and researchers valuable insight into daily behavioral trends.

This App Features

  • Brand new CWIS model: The revolutionary CWIS model makes mathematical and data based interpreteations of weather conditions based on researched bodily norms, and determines the severity of the weathers impact upon behaviours.
  • Weather display: Real-time weather display for teachers to use to determine further actions
  • Heads up display: Using an easy to read heads up display teachers can quickly determine the severity and details of the conditions and can make informed decisions on how to minimise and control student behaviours
