Introducing the Newest project: Meander Suite!

Meander Suite is a powerful research-driven platform designed to help schools better understand and manage student behavior in relation to environmental factors - Specifically the weather. Developed with researched care and innovation, it gives educators tools to predict, prepare, and prevent classroom challenges before they arise. This tool comes with an outstanding research paper that outlines the research and development of the application, and how it can be used.
