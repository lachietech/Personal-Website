Welcome To Meander Suite

Meander Suite is a complex research application developed by Nielsen Innovations that specialises in the management of the school environment, childrens behaviours, and allowing students and staff alike to be more organised and prepared.

This app takes a researched and tested response to help teachers be more informed about the behaviours of their students. Using the 3 P's model, Predict, Prepare, Prevent. Teachers can make more informed decisions on how they can work with the environmental conditions, to maximise student learning. It offers a variety of tools to help students and teachers to make these decisions and manage behaviours.
